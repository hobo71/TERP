Bird Paradise

Nature Reserve Birds’ Paradise is located on the Gdansk Sobieszewo island, at the mouth of the Bold Vistula river. The reserve includes some bodys of water, for example Birds’ Paradise Lake. This place is one of the best trails in Gdansk. Track is about 6 kilometers long. You have to reserve about 5 hours walk back and forth including some breaks. The best time to go there is from April to October, then You can see a lot of interesting species of birds.

Bolechowicka Valley

By visiting the Valley of the Bolechowicką worth extend route to the Valley of the Kobylańskiej with red cycling trail. The route is a loop. The beginning of the loop can be parking in pledged 18 hectares or Karniowicach. Loop length is ok. 7 km, and the walk does not exceed 4 hours. The trail is easy, you can meet families with prams.

Křivoklátsko Protected Landscape Area

Dense forests, massive rocks, mysterious castles and ruins as well as the wide and calm River Berounka flowing through the whole of the Křivoklátsko and Rakovnicko region – this is how you could describe one of the most beautiful regions lying on the border of Central, West and North Bohemia. Get to know this emerald gem of Europe – the Protected Landscape Area of Křivoklátsko – and discover the history of the Gothic Křivoklát Castle. In this region intersected by a great number of cycle paths and hiking routes, hops of the highest global quality and with a subtle aroma are also cultivated.
